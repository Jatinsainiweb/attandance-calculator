About AttendanceCalc

Your Trusted Companion for Attendance Tracking

Our Story

AttendanceCalc was born from a simple yet powerful idea: to help Indian college students easily track and maintain their attendance. We understand the importance of meeting the 75% attendance requirement and how it can impact your academic journey.

Our Mission

To provide students with a reliable, easy-to-use tool that helps them stay on top of their attendance requirements and achieve academic success.
